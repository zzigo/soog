acoustic function is a cross-domain emergent property or a sictth category like *ontology of functions*

So, **“rotational aerophone”** could be decomposed as:
- **material**: e.g. wood (from 1mat/physical)    
- **object**: paddle structure or flat plate (from 2obj/rotor)
- **agent**: human arm (from 3agn/human)
- **interaction**: continuous rotary motion (from 4int/excitation-modes/rotation)
- **function**: aerophonic excitation, producing sound by airflow over surface (derived at the interface level)
- 
That complexity cannot be captured cleanly if we force it into just _“acoustic materiality”_.
