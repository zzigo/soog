# Stable Audio Open Prompt Engineering for Timbre Generation

Use the following categories to structure the audio prompt. Combine one or more descriptors from each group to create a rich, intentional sound description.

## 1. Material (The physical substance)
- metal: cold, ringing, sharp, harmonic
- glass: brittle, transparent, pure, fragile
- ceramic: earthy, dry, hollow, textured
- wood: warm, organic, resonant, fibrous
- skin: soft, damped, thick, organic
- paper: thin, rustling, dry, light
- plastic: synthetic, dull, flexible, pop
- stone: heavy, massive, gritty, solid
- fiber: soft, airy, flexible, muted

## 2. Mode of Excitation (The energy source)
- struck: impulsive, impact, transient-heavy
- bowed: continuous, friction, harmonic growth
- scraped: rough, textured, noisy friction
- rubbed: soft friction, warm, continuous
- blown: airy, turbulent, breathy
- plucked: sharp attack, exponential decay
- rattled: chaotic, multiple impacts, jittery
- cracked: sudden, high-energy break, transient
- crumpled: granular, dense, multiple micro-impacts

## 3. Acoustic Features (The perceptual traits)
- bright: high spectral centroid, sharp
- dull: low-pass filtered, muted, warm
- hollow: internal resonance, tubby
- noisy: high entropy, breathy or gritty
- breathy: turbulent air, soft noise
- inharmonic: complex overtones, metallic or bell-like
- resonant: long-lasting vibration, modal
- dry: no reverb, short decay, immediate
- sustained: constant energy, flat envelope
- brittle: high frequency peaks, easily broken feel
- warm: rich lower harmonics, smooth
- rough: high sensory dissonance, granular

## 4. Temporal Morphology (The shape over time)
- sharp attack: immediate peak, percussive
- slow bloom: gradual increase in amplitude
- short decay: fast silence after peak
- unstable sustain: wobbling pitch or amplitude
- pulsed tremor: rhythmic micro-modulations
- granular burst: short, dense clouds of sound

## Prompt Construction Strategy
Combine these into a single paragraph. 
Example: "short dry percussive cedar block, low resonance, soft mallet, muted overtones, sharp attack, short decay."
Focus on isolated sound objects, no background music, no silence at the beginning. 44.1kHz quality.
