# Summary

Conceptual Summary
The Biomorphic Trumpet is a living, organic instrument that grows new harmonic series through a symbiotic relationship with its environment and performer. It features an adjustable bore that expands and contracts based on environmental moisture levels, modulating its resonance. The instrument's shell is made of a mycelium-based composite that integrates with the local ecosystem, ensuring sustainable growth and adaptability.
