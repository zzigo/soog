The central geometric form is an **Aerophone (Circle)** to represent the fundamental shape of the flute body. Modular sections like chambers and resonators are represented by smaller circles connected via arrows, indicating their attachment points and flow of air. Arrows between the main circle and these modifiable parts highlight the dynamic nature of the instrument setup.

- **Modular Connectors**: Grey to denote flexible attachment points.

**Acoustical Rationale**:

import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import numpy as np

# Plotting the organogram
plt.style.use('dark_background')

fig, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(10, 6))

# Bamboo flute main circle
ax.add_patch(plt.Circle((2.5, 4), 1.2, color='orange'))

# Resonator chamber A and B circles
ax.add_patch(plt.Circle((3.5, 4), 0.6, color='lightyellow'))
ax.add_patch(plt.Circle((4.5, 4), 0.9, color='lightyellow'))

# Modular connectors
connector1 = plt.Circle((3.2, 3.8), 0.15, color='grey')
connector2 = plt.Circle((4.8, 3.8), 0.15, color='grey')
ax.add_patch(connector1)
ax.add_patch(connector2)

# Arrows for airflow
arrow1 = plt.FancyArrowPatch((2.5, 3.9), (3.5, 4), arrowstyle='->', lw=2.5, mutation_scale=20, color='white')
arrow2 = plt.FancyArrowPatch((2.5, 3.9), (4.5, 4), arrowstyle='->', lw=2.5, mutation_scale=20, color='white')

ax.add_patch(arrow1)
ax.add_patch(arrow2)

# Labels for components
ax.text(3.6, 4.3, 'Chamber A', ha='center', va='bottom', color='black')
ax.text(4.6, 4.3, 'Chamber B', ha='center', va='bottom', color='black')

ax.spines['top'].set_visible(False)
ax.spines['right'].set_visible(False)
ax.spines['left'].set_color('black')
ax.spines['bottom'].set_color('black')
ax.grid(True, color='grey', alpha=0.3)
